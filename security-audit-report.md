# Security Audit Report 
## Finding 1: Multi-Factor Authentication Not Enabled 

Risk Level: High 

Impact: 
Unauthorized users could gain access using stolen credentials.

Recommendation: 
Enable MFA for all user accounts. 

---

## Finding 2: Weak Password Policy

Risk Level: Medium 

Impact:
Weak passwords increase the likelihood of account compromise.

Recommendation: 
Require passwords of at least 12 characters and complexity requirements.

---

## Finding 3: No Backup Testing Process 

Risk Level: High 
Impact: 
Backups may fail during a disaster recovery every event. 

Recommendation: 
Perform quarterly backup restoration tests.
